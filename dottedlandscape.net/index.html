Dotted Landscape

An interactive light installation and software project. The central piece is a light panel present in the Masters of Arts exhibition in Jätkäsaari, Helsinki.

The panel can be viewed and controlled here through the underlying interface. All actions performed here are shown in the exhibition space and vice versa.

The work is part of my master thesis on the design of interactive displays for public spaces. You can dive into the freely available code on github.
